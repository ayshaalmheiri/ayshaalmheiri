About.

On mornings that I have a few extra minutes to spare, I take a short walk to NYU Abu Dhabi's local cafe, Blacksmith, to get a warm cup of Matcha before my classes to help make my mornings even better and calmer just before classes start.


On my short journey to and from the cafe, I try to savor in the little details I encounter, that can be the smell of coffee in the air as I walk into the cafe or even the warm rays of the sun on my face. All of which bring me peace.


In this short film, I invite you to accompany me on one of my short journeys to Blacksmith - I hope you are able to savor in the details with me and feel at peace as we go about our short journey.


Let us enjoy our short journey on this sunny day :)
